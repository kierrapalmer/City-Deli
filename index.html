Who We Are

At City Deli, we have proudly served the Great Bridge area superior deli dishes for the last twenty-six years. We opened in 1990 as a humble establishment, and we strive to retain that down-to-earth nature to this day in order to provide our customers with a comfortable dining experience and make sure that they always feel at home in our deli.
