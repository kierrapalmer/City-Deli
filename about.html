Our Philosophy

Our main philosophy at City Deli is to show all of our valuable customers how much they truly mean to us. As a small business that has been running for more than twenty years off of the love and appreciation of our customers, we want to make sure that we give all of that appreciation back by providing our customers with great service and phenomenal food. We actively try to accomplish that goal by creating all of our meals from scratch with top-quality Boar Head deli meat. We also hire only the most skilled and passionate professionals to work as a part of our team and make sure that our in-house, to-go, delivery, and catering services run smoothly, quickly, and efficiently in order to ensure one hundred percent customer satisfaction.

Dining Experience

At City Deli, we offer to-go, catering, and delivery services, which many of our customers appreciate. However, we are also dedicated to providing the best possible in-house dining experience for our customers who want to stay with us a little longer. Our comfortable and homey dining area seats up to sixty-five patrons at one time. We have a strict non-smoking policy in order to ensure the comfort and cleanliness of our establishment for all of our customers.
